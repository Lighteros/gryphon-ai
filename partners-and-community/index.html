Partnership Announcements

Gryphon AI’s partners bring unique strengths to our ecosystem, and together we are building robust AI-driven solutions for Web3
Bithumb
One of Korea’s largest crypto exchanges, Bithumb supports the rollout of ELANN.AI in collaboration with Gryphon AI and Smart Valor. Together, they deliver localized AI solutions, making advanced analytics accessible to Korean users directly within their preferred platforms.
Burrito Wallet
As Korea’s leading non-custodial wallet, Burrito Wallet partnered with Gryphon AI and Smart Valor to integrate ELANN.AI. This integration allows over 400,000 Burrito Wallet users to receive real-time insights in Korean, enhancing accessibility and engagement for Korean-speaking users.
Numbers Protocol
Numbers Protocol is enhancing digital content authenticity by leveraging blockchain and AI agents to ensure provenance and transparency. Our AI agents verify and track the lifecycle of images and media, creating tamper-proof records that build trust and combat misinformation in the digital ecosystem.
Smart Valor
Smart Valor, a pioneering digital asset platform, collaborated with Gryphon AI to develop and deploy ELANN.AI, an AI-powered research agent. Smart Valor’s support and expertise helped bring ELANN.AI to life, enabling users to access tailored, real-time research insights.
ChainML
By partnering with ChainML, Gryphon AI leverages advanced machine learning to optimize on-chain data insights, delivering precise analytics for users.
OORT
Gryphon AI and OORT secure decentralized data storage, offering robust data management for AI agent interactions.
