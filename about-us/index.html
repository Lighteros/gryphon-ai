The People Behind Gryphon AI

GryphonAI brings together experts in blockchain and AI technology, backed by a history of launching successful tech ventures

United by the Mission

Gryphon AI's diverse team, spanning continents and languages, is driven by a shared mission: to create a decentralized platform where people and AI Agents collaborate in a secure permissionless way. From a machine learning engineer in the Middle East to a platform architect in Switzerland, our team embodies a potent fusion of perspectives and expertise. This synergy fuels innovation and empowers us to overcome the challenges of pioneering new frontiers.

Governed by Law and Technology

In our organizational setup, we integrate the best of two worlds - Legal foundations for clear accountability, and technological innovations for DAO-like governance. The Gryphon AI Foundation, headquartered in Liechtenstein, acts as the official legal entity representing the interests of our non-profit open platform. Committed to open-source principles, our foundation strives to attract the leading minds in both AI and blockchain.
