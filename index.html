What is Gryphon AI?

Gryphon AI is where the strength of a lion (blockchain) meets the wings of an eagle (AI).
Easily use, create, train, deploy and market intelligent AI assistants in a secure interface

Just like our namesake, at Gryphon AI we combine two powerful entities, bringing the power of AI to the decentralized world of blockchain

THE PROBLEM:
Web2’s centralized AI limits user autonomy, operates within closed systems transacting within traditional banking system.
THE OPPORTUNITY:
Blockchain-powered, autonomous agents that thrive in decentralized applications (dApps), create a more innovative ecosystem open to all.

Gryphon AI’s Architecture

Gryphon AI is an on-chain network and Agent Environment. Our Network assigns each agent an ID,  permissionless access and transaction capabilities
  • UTILIZE agents in the AI Playground
  • CREATE custom agents with the AI Builder
  • MONETIZE agents in the AI Marketplace

Gryphon AI Network

Gryphon AI Network has four key components which together empower both technical and non-technical founders to build, deploy, and monetize AI agents for Web3
Decentralized AI Network
Join the decentralized network - contribute models, prompt libraries, datasets, or become a node operator to secure the Gryphon AI protocol.
Registry & Reputation System
Gryphon AI’s registry ensures verifiable and immutable blockchain records, while our Reputation System lets participants rate agents for transparency and trust.
Authentication & Payment
AI agents will autonomously handle on-chain operations like authentication and payments, secured by the decentralized blockchain.
AI Agent Playground
Test and refine early AI agents in the Gryphon AI Playground today. Soon, you’ll be able to collaborate and build your own agents.

How the Gryphon AI Network Operates

AI solution providers and agent developers run nodes on the Gryphon Network. Crypto projects, dApps and other users pay nodes to use their AI solutions.

Who is Gryphon AI Builder for?

AI Infrastructure Providers:

Catering to companies, projects, and individuals offering LLMs, prompt libraries, and computational resources
EXAMPLE
A project that develops its own fine-tuned version of the Meta Llama 3 Large Language Model.
ADVANTAGES
Provide your model to developers permissionlessly, without the need for any third-party or corporate approval.

Agent Developers:

Technical teams develop powerful AI agents.
EXAMPLE
A project which develops a complex agent, such as a crypto research analyst
ADVANTAGES
  • Enable your agent to independently handle transactions like purchasing research reports or accessing specific data upon a user request​.
  • Gain revenue and reputation by offering access to your agent to Web3 users.

Agent Creators:

Enabling non-technical users to easily create and deploy customized AI agents, and enabling more complex coding options for devs
EXAMPLE
DeFi user from Argentina building a Spanish-speaking research analyst agent focused exclusively on DeFi projects​
ADVANTAGES
  • Quickly develop your agent with an intuitive low-code interface.
  • Provide paid access to Web3 users and build your reputation.

dApps
