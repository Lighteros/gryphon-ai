FAQ

What are AI Agents?
AI agents are intelligent programs that autonomously perform complex tasks. They interact with other agents, utilize applications, and collaborate with humans to achieve specific goals.
AI Agents possess the following key characteristics:
  • autonomy
    Operating independently, making decisions and performing actions without continual human oversight.
  • Reactivity & Proactivity
    Reacting to environmental changes while proactively pursuing goals.
  • Learning & Adaptation
    Learning from experiences and adapting to new situations to enhance performance.
Why are AI agents better on-chain?
AI agents are better on-chain because no corporation can control them. Agents are becoming more autonomous and useful, being able to transact independently and have their own wallets.
Our platform is:
  • Decentralized Trust
    Operating within secure, distributed systems, AI agents eliminate single points of control, reducing risks of tampering or interference by corporates or governments.
  • Financial Autonomy
    ‍    With native crypto wallets, AI agents can transact, pay fees, and participate in decentralized economies independently, enhancing their self-sufficiency.
  • Transparency:
    ‍    AI agents on-chain leverage blockchain's immutable records, ensuring every action and decision is fully verifiable and fostering unparalleled trust.
